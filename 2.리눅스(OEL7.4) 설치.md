**2.3 리눅스 내부 설정**

![image](https://github.com/oraclejyp/19c_rac_inst/assets/133745372/0dc926df-24ca-4537-8422-082d1d97ab84)

![image](https://github.com/oraclejyp/19c_rac_inst/assets/133745372/43148f7b-cec8-4556-a45a-3ff9d9268dfb)
